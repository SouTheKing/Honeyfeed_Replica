Driven To The Hell

Synopsis

Marlik Tuwed, the puppeteer who nearly doomed Earth, is dead. Or so the world believes. Reborn in another world as Basalin Lenard, a lion beast-kin, he remembers nothing of his dark past, but his presence remains inside. As shadows of destiny are stirring, the Demon Lord has risen, hellbent on annihilating elves, dwarves, humans, and beast-kin and this time, he actually succeeded. As the world stands on the brink of ruin, Basalin uncovers the truth about his past… and decides whether to embrace the monster he once was or become the hero the world desperately needs.

Nichigetsu Kosen

Synopsis

With the Sun and Moon being the main sources of flux, the whole world got divided between Solars and Lunars. Where Solar flux offers physical capabilities, Lunar flux specialises in mental abilities. But a third entity always haunted the Earth, forcing everything into destruction. Another source of flux—Black Holes. Though little, its tenebral flux has been mutating the living, all and forever, granting them powers to destroy. One day, Arata Tsukihara, a seventeen-year-old boy, gets confronted by such beings. The secrets that were kept hidden from the people finally surfaced before him, changing his entire view of the society. With nothing left to look back on, he intertwines himself further as he sets out on a journey led by a mysterious card.

Our Final Rest

Synopsis

Four centuries ago, the prodigy Ariellea Dros sealed herself away after her apocalyptic creations, the "Forbiddens", brought the world to the brink of annihilation. Now, she is awakened by Caelin Lenyar, a hardened young man cursed with a terrifying power that makes him a feared pariah. Caelin’s goal is absolute: gather the scattered Forbiddens and use their catastrophic might to tear the world apart. Bound to him by duty and an inescapable magical link, Ariellea becomes his reluctant guide. Alongside some fellow outcasts, they begin a grim hunt across the fractured world of Orvanys.
